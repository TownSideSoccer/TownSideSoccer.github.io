About Our Club

Town Side Soccer Club (TSS Club), based in Reengus, Sikar (Rajasthan), was established with a mission to uplift grassroots football and provide world-class training to young, passionate players from town and rural areas. Our vision is to build a vibrant footballing community that nurtures talent, promotes discipline, and encourages physical and mental development through sport.

We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to play, grow, and compete at the highest level — regardless of their background. With a team of experienced coaches and a structured training curriculum, TSS Club focuses on skill development, teamwork, and sportsmanship. From foundational training to competitive matches and tournaments, our club offers a platform for every aspiring player to shine.

Our journey is powered by the values of dedication, unity, respect, and resilience. We’re more than just a football academy — we are a family committed to shaping future champions both on and off the field.
